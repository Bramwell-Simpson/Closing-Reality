Closing Reality

Closing Reality is a project about logging out of the everyday mundane life that we call reality by destroying the perceptions contained in it through audio and visual experience.

This project isn't contained to one genre but focuses primarily on house/dnb/bass genres of electronic music.

The members of Closing Reality include the sole owner, Kyle from Boise, ID.

I outsource to friends and artists across the web to help make the project a new and unique reality for everyone to escape into and enjoy!

Always be sure to check out any friends that are linked in my works that I collaborate with!

Thanks for reading about my project. I want to create a universe for everyone to escape into when they need it
